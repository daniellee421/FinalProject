The Racing Bar Chart of College Powerhouses

This racing bar chart of colleges demonstrates how many of their players get drafted into the NBA from 1985 to 2013. From 1985 to 1995, the University of North Carolina and Arizona were the two top schools with the highest number of NBA players. Arizona had 19 players in the league, while UNC just tailed behind at 18. From 1995 to 2005, UNC continued to lead with 29 players in the NBA. UCLA caught up into the race and tied in with UNC, also with a total of 29 players. Arizona went down a step to third place with a total of 28 players. But from 2005 to 2013, Kentucky, the world renown college basketball powerhouse led by Coach Calipari, claimed first place by a huge margin. They sent a total of 55 players to the NBA, 21 of their players being from 2005 to 2013. Duke, also famous for being the embodiment of a student-athlete school, sent a total of 46 players to the NBA led by Coach K. North Carolina continued to send many players tot he NBA despite falling to third place with a total of 42 players.
